About Pirate Crew

We are a group of students working on this website as a project. Our goal is to learn and share knowledge about community engagement while building a simple and fun website together.

1. Why We Made This

We wanted to create a website that explains community engagement in a simple way. It’s a way for us to practice web design and teamwork while helping others understand how communities work.

2. What We Did

We built pages with information, pictures, and links to make it easy to learn about community engagement. Each part of the site is designed to show how students can work together and share knowledge.

3. What We Learned

Through this project, we practiced planning, coding, and presenting information online. We also learned how to work as a team and make something useful that others can see and enjoy.
